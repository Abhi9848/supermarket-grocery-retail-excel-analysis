# supermarket-grocery-retail-excel-analysis
Overview
This repository contains an in-depth Excel analysis of supermarket and grocery retail data from 2015â€“2018. It explores sales, profits, product and category trends, customer segments, and geographic breakdowns to uncover actionable insights for retail business growth.

Dataset
Rows: Transactions from 2015-2018

Columns: Order ID, Customer Name, Category, Sub-Category, City, State, Region, Order Date, Sales, Discount, Profit, Profit Margin

Source: Publicly available retail dataset (or your data source)

Key Features
Yearly & Region-Wise Trends: Discover growth patterns and compare market regions.

Product & Category Ranking: Identify top-selling products and high-profit categories.

Seasonality: Monthly and yearly analysis to spot trends and optimal periods for business.

Customer Segmentation: Analysis by Regular, Member, or Online customers.

Profit Margin, Discounts & Sales: Find deals driving profits and those needing review.

Insights
Profits grew each year, with 2018 as the peak.

Eggs/Meat/Fish, Snacks, and Grains drove major sales.

Top sales regions: West, East, Central. Key cities: Kanyakumari, Vellore, Bodi.

Discounts affected monthly profit margins; recommendations for improved pricing and promotions are provided.
